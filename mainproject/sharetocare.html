Share-to-Care

a family to family platform that makes sure donations can be delivered directly into the hands of those families in need.

SharetoCare aims at linking abundant families that have more to give, to families in need of kids clothing, toys and books

    Our goals are

  • Minimize plastic waste caused by the toy industry

    • Did you know that 90% of kids toys are made of plastics, but 80% of all toys end up in trash heap, and it takes up to 1000 years to decompose plastics in landfill. For every 1 million the toy industry makes, 40 tons of plastics are used.

  • Reusing second-hand clothes, toys and books

    • We want to help parents who don't know what to do with all the outgrown clothes and toys;We want to help families who works hard to put food on the table for their kids, but can afford only that

  • Feed those hungry minds that are so eager to learn
  • Preserve a better environment for our future generations
